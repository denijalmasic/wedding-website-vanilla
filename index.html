SStages of work

  1. 1Acquaintance

    Our acquaintance will begin with a call or a completed form, after which we will arrange a meeting in our office or a Skype call.

  2. 2First meeting

    We will discuss the wishes and vision of the wedding day, decide on the date, venue of the celebration and make an estimate.

  3. 3Conclusion of an agreement

    We approve the concept of the celebration and a detailed plan for the wedding. After discussing the project, we conclude and agreement and start work.

  1. 4Wedding preparation

    This process includes the selection of a site, the selection of suppliers, the approval of estimates and the development of all details.

  2. 5Wedding preparations

    If you want to make sure everything goes to plan we will organize a wedding day rehearsal.

  3. 6Wedding Day

    We control the entire process, solving all the associated tasks and problems. Trust us and enjoy the celebration.
